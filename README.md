# Privacy Policy for JobQue

This Privacy Policy describes how JobQue ("the App" or "we") handles your information. JobQue is designed to help users find employment opportunities efficiently while prioritizing user privacy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

### A. Personal Account Information
JobQue is an account-free application. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. There is no sign-up or registration process required to use the App.

### B. Search Queries and Data Transmission
To provide you with job search results, the App requires an active internet connection to communicate with third-party service providers via API (Application Programming Interface).

- **Data Transmitted:**  
  When you perform a search, the criteria you provide (such as job title, company name, and location) are transmitted to a third-party API to fetch relevant job listings.

- **Purpose:**  
  This data is used solely to return real-time job opportunities to your device.

- **Retention:**  
  We do not store your search history on our servers. Search parameters are used in real-time and are not linked to any personal identity.

### C. External Links
The App provides links to third-party websites (e.g., company career pages or job boards) where you can apply for positions.

- **Policy Note:**  
  Once you click a link and leave the App, you are subject to the privacy policy of that external website. We do not control and are not responsible for the data collection practices of these third-party sites.

### D. Local Storage
JobQue may store minimal configuration data locally on your device (such as your last searched location or app preferences) using your device's local storage (e.g., AsyncStorage). This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

We use Expo development tools and RapidAPI to facilitate the App’s core functionality.

- **Service Providers:**  
  These services may process non-personal data (such as IP addresses or device identifiers) to manage API requests, ensure app stability, and prevent service abuse.

- **No Sale of Data:**  
  We do not sell, rent, or trade your search data or device information to third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since JobQue does not use accounts or server-side databases for user profiles, we do not retain any of your data. If you wish to clear your local search history or preferences, you can do so by clearing the App's cache in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

---

## 4. Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All communication between the App and the API is conducted via encrypted HTTPS (TLS) connections. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.


---

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our official website. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
